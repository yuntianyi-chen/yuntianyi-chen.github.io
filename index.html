Biography

Yuntianyi Chen is a Ph.D. student of software engineering at University of California, Irvine. His research interests include include automated testing of autonomous vehicles, performance ranking of software configuration, and transfer learning. He joined the Software Aurora Lab (SORA) since 2021. His advisor is Prof. Joshua Garcia.
